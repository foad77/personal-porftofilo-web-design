About

As a seasoned professional in the fields of data science, power system engineering, embedded systems, and full-stack development, my academic journey has been foundational to my success. I hold an undergraduate degree in Power Systems, a Master's in Power Electronics, and a Ph.D. in Systems Engineering, with a specific focus on energy management for smart grids. This educational background has equipped me with a deep understanding of both theoretical and practical aspects of energy systems, enabling me to innovate and solve complex problems efficiently. I excel in applying machine learning techniques to improve energy forecast models and have a strong track record in hardware optimization. My expertise is broad, encompassing optimization algorithms, programming in languages such as Python and C/C++, and leveraging web development technologies for comprehensive solutions. I have contributed significantly to the academic community, authoring peer-reviewed articles and serving as a reviewer, demonstrating my dedication to advancing the energy sector. My skills are not just confined to technical abilities but also include a keen sense for innovative solutions, making me a valuable asset in seeking to drive forward advancements in energy management and smart grid technologies.
